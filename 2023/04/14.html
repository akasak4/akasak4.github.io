Oshi no Ko (推しの子)

Posted on April 14, 2023 18:56 AM

The anime premiered yesterday, and there were so many people talking about it on social media like Facebook, Twitter, or something. So, I decided to watch it yesterday.

Before the anime aired, my friend actually suggested that I should watch it, but I rejected the idea because I misunderstood and thought it was a light novel that I had already read. (The title of the light novel is Kimi wa Hontouni Boku no Tenshi nano ka)

And yeah, I watched the anime. At first, I thought it would be boring, but guess what? I was wrong. This anime is really great, and I'm not lying. It's a fresh anime, there's no isekai or something like that. Everything is new and fresh. The story is amazing. I've never read the manga before, but I'm totally impressed with this anime.

Can't wait for the next episode!
