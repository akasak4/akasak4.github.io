2013 and 2014

Posted on July 26, 2023 21:18 PM

For me, there is quite a differences that distinguishes the years 2013 and 2014. It feels like there is a border that separates these years into different eras. I don't know why, but I feels like that something has changed, especially in the technology field. Just think about how the internet speed back then. it was really horrible. Downloading files from the internet used to be painfully slow, and the internet cost was quite expensive. and if you watch some youtube videos or streaming videos on some particular site it's always buffering. It was a nightmare to live in that time.

Additionally, there was a transformation from Blackberry or Symbian phones to Android smartphones. I still remember how people used to exchange their Blackberry PIN numbers. Those were good old days. Although I didn't use it often at that time, I preferred Line Messaging over Blackberry. Because an ID Username is easier to memorize than a PIN.

While many people might argue that the years 2014 and 2015 actually distinguish these years into a different era, I personally believe that 2014 had a more modern feeling than 2013, making it a suitable year to compare these eras.
